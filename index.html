ServoLink

ServoLink is an advanced prosthetic arm that captures real-time motion from a human hand and mimics it using servo-driven actuators. It utilizes motion sensors (like flex sensors or IMUs) worn on the user's real hand to track finger and wrist movement, which are then replicated by a 3D-printed mechanical arm. Powered by a microcontroller.
